About The Penalty Oracle

The Penalty Oracle is a free browser game that turns any yes-or-no question into a penalty shootout. Stuck on a decision? Wondering whether your team will win, whether to order pizza, or whether to text them back? Ask the question out loud, step up to the spot, and let your aim decide. It is a light-hearted twist on the classic "magic answer" toys — no sign-up, no download, and it works on phones and desktops.

How to Play

  1. Say your yes-or-no question out loud, for example "Will Spain beat Japan?"
  2. You get five penalty kicks. Each turn, pick a target by tapping one of the five zones on the goal: top left, top right, middle, bottom left, or bottom right.
  3. The goalkeeper dives to guess where you shot. Beat him and it is a goal; guess the same spot and it is a save. He can also make fingertip saves on nearby shots, and some shots fly off target.
  4. Score 3 or more of your 5 kicks and the Oracle answers YES. Score fewer and the answer is NO.
  5. Press Ask Again to pose a new question.

Tips Against the Brutal Keeper

This keeper is deliberately tough, so a YES has to be earned. A few things help:

  • The middle is risky — the keeper can reach it from almost any dive, so use it sparingly.
  • The corners beat the keeper more often, but blast too wildly and the ball sails off target.
  • There is no perfect strategy — that uncertainty is the whole point of asking the Oracle.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really random?

The keeper's dive and whether a shot stays on target are decided randomly each kick, so no two shootouts play out the same way. It is a game of chance for fun — please do not use it for important decisions.

Does it cost anything?

No. The Penalty Oracle is completely free to play. The site is supported by advertising.

Do I need to install anything?

No. It runs entirely in your web browser on mobile or desktop.
